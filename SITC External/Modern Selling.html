Modern Selling

The evolution of IT resellers

The dynamics of B2B sales has changed the way you have to sell and interact with your customers.

Meet the powerful platform powering modern selling in the digital economy.

arrow_drop_down

B2B buyers are increasingly digitally savvy and self-educated. They prefer to not interact with a salesperson when gathering product or service information, tracking deliveries and shipping times, and buying a product or service.

59% of B2B buyers prefer not to interact with humans via phone.

90% of B2B buyers prefer to buy online when they have decided what to buy.

Adapt or Decline

Amazon business is taking more and more market share. They are now going after B2B.

However as a reseller you have key differentiators and value add. Embracing the strategies listed below and moving to a modern selling strategy will enable you to improve customer experience and drives profitable growth.

The Principles Of Modern Selling

Stock in the channel have defined 7 keys principles you must adopt to become modern sellers.

01

Self-Service

Digital natives and millennials don’t like talking on the phone. They want and expect to be able to perform routine tasks themselves, whilst interacting with as few people as possible.

Buyers have evolved, and their experiences at home, are shaping their expectations in the B2B world.

02

Collaboration

Sales as a side-by-side process, instead of head-on. Online spaces to work on projects and quotes that can be shared and amended in real time speed up the sales cycle.

Letting users go straight to chat, video or voice directly from your web page enables true working together.

03

Self-Education

Let them find out about your solutions on their terms. Expose price information early in the journey.

Provide wizards to guide them plus bundles and pre-set configurations for easier decision making.

04

Personalisation

To deliver a great experience, businesses must offer a unique, personalized journey by using their understanding of customers.

Offer custom catalogues and pricing, relevant add-on products and personalised offers.

05

Transparency

Above everything, build trust with consistent pricing and messages through all channels.

Communicate timelines instantly, explain your solutions simply and provide deep and accurate descriptions of your products and services.

06

Automation

Make key people more efficient, remove repetitive tasks, communicate electronically with your suppliers, automate service provision and delivery.

Synchronise information across systems giving you, your sales people and your customers access to what they need to know.

07

Insight

Using data analysis to spot patterns, opportunities or problems and to develop a deep understanding of customers.

Real-time insights allow you to reach out at just the right time to enable your sales goals.

Become A Modern Seller Is Hard

It is difficult to change to this new world. Our comprehensive toolset will enable you.

To mention just one feature, collaborative quoting, think how much better your business would be if you had quotes and projects online - with shared spaces to add resources and details that can be edited in real time - with the sales people informed the customer is looking at the project and popping up chat, or even moving to video or voice directly from your web page.

With just this, one of many tools, think how much more effective your sales process could become.
